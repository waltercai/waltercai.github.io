Brief Bio

I am a PhD student member of the Databases group in the University of Washington Computer Science & Engineering department. I am fortunate to be working with Professors Dan Suciu, and Magdalena Balazinska.

Previously, I studied mathematics at Cornell University, graduating summa cum laude in 2014. During my undergrad I participated in two REU's at Rutgers DIMACS, and UCLA IPAM. Following my bachelor's I attended the University of Wisconsin completing a Master's in CS where I worked with Prof. AnHai Doan. Before coming to Seattle I interned for Bayesian machine learning startup, Gamalon.
