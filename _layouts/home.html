Brief Bio
I am a graduate student at the University of Washington in the Computer Science & Engineering department. Specifically, I am a member of the UW Database group where I am fortunate to be working with Prof. Dan Suciu, and Prof. Magdalena Balazinska. I previously studied mathematics at Cornell University where I graduated in 2014. During my undergrad I participated in two REU's at Rutgers DIMACS, and UCLA IPAM. Following my bachelor's I attended the University of Wisconsin completing a Masters in CS where I worked with Prof. AnHai Doan. Before coming to Seattle I interned for Bayesian machine learning startup Gamalon.
